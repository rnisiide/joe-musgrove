Antarctica with Joe Musgrove

Join Joe Musgrove on this unforgettable trip to Antarctica to support BeTheMatch.org

Anyone who knows Joe would agree that other than baseball and coffee, Joe's two passions in life are connecting with his fans and supporting his community.

San Diego native No-no Joe and Antarctica Travel Group have teamed up to offer an unforgettable experience to Antarctica to raise funds and awareness for BeTheMatch.org

A portion of all sales for this trip as well as 100% of the onboard fundraising auction will be donated directly to BeTheMatch.org.
